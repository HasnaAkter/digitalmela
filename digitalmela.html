Digital Bangladesh Mela 2020

16-18 January

Digital Bangladesh Mela 2020 is the largest exposition aimed at showcasing IT and ITES products and services in Bangladesh is going to be held from Jan 16, 2020 - Jan 18, 2020.

It will focus on creating more collaborative relationships at national and international level, inviting trade delegations to experience the immense potentials of Bangladeshi ICT industry.

As the country has taken a great leap in Digital Wave, we do believe that the three-day event this year will be a congregation of ICT companies, personalities, professionals, key policy makers, industry leaders and other ICT stakeholders.
